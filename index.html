About Me

Hi! My name is Kurt Roy. I am an audio engineer living in Seattle, Washington. I have more than ten years' experience recording, mixing, and mastering. My work has seen over 3 million streams on Spotify and Apple Music.

I first became interested in audio engineering after realizing that making high quality music requires much more than than simply being a good musician. As an indie/bedroom-pop artist, being able to DIY all stages of music production became especially important to me.

I would love the opportunity to work on your single, EP, or full album. In my home studio, I use high quality converters, studio monitors, and outboard gear to ensure top-tier professionalism. (For more details regarding the technical specifications of the gear I use, feel free to message me.) Your satisfication as an artist is my top concern, so I offer unlimited mix revisions.

For my current availability and anticipated turnaround time, send me an email with the details of your request and I'll get back to you right away. Thanks for your interest - it means the world.
